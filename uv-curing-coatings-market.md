<p><ul><li>Monomers</li><li>Oligomers</li><li>Photoinitiators</li><li>PU Dispersions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV curing coatings market is categorized into four types: monomers, oligomers, photoinitiators, and PU dispersions. Monomers are small molecules that contribute to the final coating properties. Oligomers are larger molecules that provide the coating's backbone structure. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate the UV curing process. PU dispersions consist of polyurethane particles dispersed in water and are used in coatings for their durability and flexibility. Each type plays a crucial role in the performance and characteristics of UV curing coatings.</p></p>
