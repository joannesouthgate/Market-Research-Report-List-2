<p><p>Dry whole milk powder market comprises various types including regular, instant, UHT, caramelized, and organic products. Regular milk powder is the standard form of dry whole milk, while instant milk powder dissolves quickly in water. UHT milk powder undergoes ultra-high temperature processing for longer shelf life. Caramelized milk powder is sweetened with caramel flavor, while organic milk powder is made from milk produced without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and demands in the market.</p></p>
