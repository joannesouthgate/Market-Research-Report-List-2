<p><p>LCD Monitor Arm market consists of three key types: Keyhole, Fixture, and Wall Hanging. Keyhole types require drilling holes into the wall or desk for installation, providing a sturdy and fixed mount. Fixture types clamp onto the edge of a desk or table, offering flexibility and easy adjustment. Wall Hanging types are mounted directly onto the wall, maximizing space and providing a sleek look. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, offering various options for users to choose from in the market.</p></p>
