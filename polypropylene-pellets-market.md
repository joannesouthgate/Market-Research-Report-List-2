<p><p>Polypropylene pellets are divided into two main market types based on their density: low density and high density. Low density polypropylene pellets are lightweight, flexible, and have good impact resistance, making them ideal for applications such as packaging, textiles, and consumer goods. On the other hand, high density polypropylene pellets are more rigid, durable, and have a higher melting point, making them suitable for use in products that require strength and heat resistance, such as automotive parts and industrial containers.</p></p>
