<p><p>Biodegradable polyester market includes homopolymers such as PHA, PHB, PLA, and PCL, as well as copolymers like PBS and PHBV. Homopolymers are made of a single type of repeating unit and exhibit specific properties based on their composition. Copolymers, on the other hand, are composed of two or more different types of repeating units, offering a combination of properties from each individual unit. Both homopolymers and copolymers play a crucial role in the growing market for biodegradable polyester alternatives.</p></p>
