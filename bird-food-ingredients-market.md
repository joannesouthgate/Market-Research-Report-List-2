<p><p>Bird food ingredients market can be broadly categorized into two types - shelled type and unshelled type. Shelled type bird food includes seeds and nuts that have been removed from their shells, making it easier for birds to consume. On the other hand, unshelled type bird food consists of whole seeds and nuts that are still in their shells, providing birds with a more challenging eating experience. Both types cater to different bird species and preferences, offering a variety of options for bird owners to choose from.</p></p>
