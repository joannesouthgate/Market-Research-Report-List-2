<p><p>Multi-feed check scanners come in two market types: those that are Ultraviolet (UV) capable and those that are not. The UV capable models are equipped with ultraviolet sensors that can detect UV security features on checks to prevent fraud. These models are ideal for businesses that deal with a high volume of checks and prioritize security. On the other hand, the models without UV capabilities are more cost-effective and suitable for businesses that do not require enhanced security measures.</p></p>
