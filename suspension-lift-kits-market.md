<p><p>Suspension Lift Kits come in various types such as Spring Suspension Lift Kits, which use longer coil springs to increase ride height; Coil Suspension Lift Kits, which involve replacing the entire coil spring to achieve a lift; Shackle Suspension Lift Kits, which involve swapping out stock shackles for longer ones to raise the vehicle's height. Other types of suspension lift kits may include torsion bar lifts or spacer lifts. Each type of lift kit offers different levels of lift and customization options for off-road enthusiasts.</p></p>
