<p><p>Explosion-proof oil filters are designed to safely remove impurities from oil in hazardous environments. There are two main types of explosion-proof oil filters on the market. Vacuum type filters operate by creating a vacuum to draw oil through a filter element, removing impurities. Centrifugal type filters use centrifugal force to separate solids from liquids in the oil. Both types of filters are designed to prevent explosions in environments where flammable gases or vapors may be present, providing a safer working environment for operators.</p></p>
