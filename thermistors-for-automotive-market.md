<p><strong>The Thermistors for Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTC Type</li><li>NTC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermistors in the automotive market can be classified into two main types: PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) thermistors. PTC thermistors have a resistance that increases with temperature, while NTC thermistors have a resistance that decreases with temperature. PTC thermistors are commonly used for overcurrent protection and temperature compensation applications, while NTC thermistors are often used for temperature sensing and control in automotive systems such as engine cooling and climate control.</p></p>
