<p><p>General grade chip ferrite beads are classified into different types based on their impedance levels. Beads with less than 100 Ohms impedance are suitable for high-frequency applications where noise suppression is crucial. Those with impedance ranging from 100 to 1000 Ohms are commonly used in filters and signal line applications. Beads with impedance greater than 1000 Ohms are ideal for power lines and DC/DC converters due to their high impedance properties. These different types cater to various needs in the market for effective noise suppression and signal filtering.</p></p>
