<p><p>Control release fertilizers are categorized into three main types: Synthetic organic nitrogen fertilizers, coated fertilizers, and others. Synthetic organic nitrogen fertilizers are chemically synthesized fertilizers containing nitrogen for slow release. Coated fertilizers are granules encapsulated in a polymer coating that breaks down over time, releasing nutrients gradually. Other control release fertilizers include sulfur-coated urea and polymer-coated fertilizers. These types of fertilizers are designed to provide a controlled release of nutrients to plants, ensuring optimal absorption and usage.</p></p>
