<p><p>Remote control switches come in various types such as Zigbee, WiFi, RF 315M, and others. Zigbee remote control switches use a mesh network for communication, providing low power consumption and long-range capabilities. WiFi switches connect to an existing WiFi network for remote access through a smartphone. RF 315M switches operate on a specific radio frequency for wireless control. Other types of remote control switches may include Bluetooth or Infrared options, each offering unique features and benefits to suit different user preferences.</p></p>
