<p><strong>The Remolded Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Remolded tires, also known as retread tires, can be classified into two main market types: Pre Cure and Mold Cure. Pre Cure retreading involves bonding a new tread to a tire casing before placing it in a mold to cure. Mold Cure retreading, on the other hand, involves applying a uncured tread directly to a tire casing and then placing it in a mold to cure. Both methods offer a cost-effective and environmentally friendly alternative to purchasing new tires.</p></p>
