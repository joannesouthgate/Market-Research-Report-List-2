<p><p>Semiconductor Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) Equipment Market is categorized into three types based on pressure levels during the deposition process. Atmospheric Pressure CVD operates at ambient pressure, suitable for large-scale production. Low-pressure CVD involves reduced pressure levels, improving film quality and uniformity. Ultrahigh Vacuum CVD takes place at extremely low pressure levels, ensuring high-purity film deposition. Each type caters to specific requirements in semiconductor manufacturing, providing options for different scales of production and film quality needs.</p></p>
