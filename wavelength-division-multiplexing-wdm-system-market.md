<p><strong>The Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coarse Wavelength-division Multiplexing (CWDM)</li><li>Dense Wavelength-division Multiplexing (DWDM).</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) systems allow multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single fiber optic connection. Coarse Wavelength-division Multiplexing (CWDM) uses fewer wavelengths with larger intervals, making it more cost-effective for shorter distances. Dense Wavelength-division Multiplexing (DWDM) utilizes closely spaced wavelengths to increase the capacity and efficiency of long-distance transmissions. Both CWDM and DWDM systems cater to different market needs based on the distance and capacity requirements of the network.</p></p>
