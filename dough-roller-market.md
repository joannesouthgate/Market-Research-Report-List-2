<p><p>Dough rollers come in two main types: tabletop and floor standing. Tabletop dough rollers are smaller and more compact, making them ideal for small businesses or home use. They are also easier to move around and store. On the other hand, floor standing dough rollers are larger and more heavy-duty, making them suitable for high-volume commercial kitchens or bakeries. They are designed to handle larger batches of dough and are more durable for continuous use. Both types of dough rollers serve the same purpose of efficiently and evenly rolling out dough for various culinary applications.</p></p>
