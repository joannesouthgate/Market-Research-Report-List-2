<p><p>UHMWPE fabric market consists of three main types - woven fabric, knitted fabric, and non-woven fabric. Woven fabric is created by interlacing yarns in a crisscross pattern, providing durability and strength. Knitted fabric is made by looping yarns together, offering flexibility and stretch. Non-woven fabric is produced by bonding fibers together, resulting in a lightweight and cost-effective option. Each type of fabric has its own unique characteristics and applications, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
