<p><p>The Electric Fencing market includes two main types: Permanent Fence and Portable Fence. Permanent Fencing is installed for long-term use in areas where a permanent barrier is required, such as around properties or farms. Portable Fencing, on the other hand, is designed for temporary or movable applications, such as for rotational grazing or construction sites. Both types offer security and protection by delivering electric shocks to deter animals or intruders, but they differ in terms of their durability and flexibility.</p></p>
