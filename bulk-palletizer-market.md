<p><p>Low level palletizers are typically used for smaller scale operations where the height of the process is limited and manual loading of product onto pallets is feasible. High level palletizers, on the other hand, are designed for larger scale operations where products are automatically loaded onto pallets at higher elevations. Low level palletizers are more cost-effective for smaller businesses, while high level palletizers are more efficient for larger production facilities. Both types offer automation and efficiency benefits for palletizing operations.</p></p>
