<p><p>Compact solid-state LIDAR technology is advancing rapidly with three main market types emerging. MEMS-based scanning LIDAR systems use tiny mirrors to steer laser beams for precise mapping and sensing. Phased array LIDARs utilize multiple antennas to steer beams electronically, offering fast and accurate imaging. Non-scanning flash LIDARs illuminate the entire scene at once for quick data capture. Each market type has its unique strengths, making them suitable for various applications such as autonomous vehicles, robotics, and smart cities.</p></p>
