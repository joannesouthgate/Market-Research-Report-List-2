<p><ul><li>Crystal Material</li><li>Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IR windows are commonly classified into two main types based on the material used, crystal and polymer. Crystal materials such as ZnSe, ZnS, and sapphire are known for their high transmittance and durability, making them suitable for high temperature and harsh environment applications. On the other hand, polymer materials like polyethylene and polypropylene offer good chemical resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for applications where weight and cost are a concern. Both types of IR windows have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
