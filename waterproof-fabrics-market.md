<p><p>Waterproof fabrics market is segmented into two main types: breathable and non-breathable. Breathable waterproof fabrics are designed to allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing water from entering, making them ideal for outdoor activities. Non-breathable waterproof fabrics are tightly woven materials that do not allow any air or moisture to pass through, providing full protection against water but can cause discomfort during physical activities. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
