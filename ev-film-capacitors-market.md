<p><strong>The EV Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Film Capacitors</li><li>DC Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC Film Capacitors are specifically designed for alternating current applications, providing high capacitance stability and low dielectric losses. They are commonly used in power supplies, motor run applications, and lighting ballasts. On the other hand, DC Film Capacitors are designed for direct current applications, offering high insulation resistance and low leakage current. They are widely used in filtering, coupling, timing, and bypassing applications in various electronic devices. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable operation of electronic systems.</p></p>
