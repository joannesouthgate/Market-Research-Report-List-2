<p><p>Short glass fiber thermoplastic market includes two main types: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves melting the thermoplastic resin and injecting it into a mold to produce a desired shape. This process is commonly used for mass production of complex parts with high accuracy. On the other hand, extrusion molding involves forcing the molten resin through a die to create continuous shapes such as pipes, tubes, and sheets. Both processes offer cost-effective solutions for manufacturing lightweight and durable products.</p></p>
