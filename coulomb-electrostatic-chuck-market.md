<p><p>Coulomb Electrostatic Chuck is used for holding and positioning substrates in various industries. In the unipolar market type, the chuck uses a single polarity attracting the substrate towards it. In contrast, the multipolar market type utilizes multiple polarities to hold the substrate firmly. The unipolar chuck provides a simpler design and is suitable for basic applications, while the multipolar chuck offers stronger holding force and is preferred for more complex and demanding tasks. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
