<p><ul><li>Mono Tube Gas Charged Shock Absorber</li><li>Twin Tube Gas Charged Shock Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heavy truck gas charged shock absorbers come in two main types: Mono Tube and Twin Tube. </p><p>1. Mono Tube Gas Charged Shock Absorbers: These shocks feature a single tube design, allowing for larger piston and oil capacity. They provide better heat dissipation and are more responsive, making them ideal for heavy-duty vehicles that require performance and durability.</p><p>2. Twin Tube Gas Charged Shock Absorbers: These shocks have an inner and outer tube, with the inner tube housing the piston and oil. They are cost-effective and offer a comfortable ride, making them suitable for regular heavy truck usage.</p></p>
