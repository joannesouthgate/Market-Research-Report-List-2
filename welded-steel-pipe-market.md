<p><p>Welded steel pipe market can be classified into two main types based on the processing technique: cold drawing and hot rolling. Cold drawing involves pulling the steel tube through a die to form the desired shape, resulting in a smoother surface finish and tighter dimensional tolerances. On the other hand, hot rolling involves heating the steel billet and passing it through rollers to achieve the desired diameter and shape. Both techniques have their advantages and are used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
