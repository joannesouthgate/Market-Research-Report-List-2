<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor</li><li>Asynchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Vehicle Motors Market includes two major types of motors - Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor and Asynchronous Motor. Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor operates by maintaining a constant magnetic field between the stator and rotor, resulting in higher efficiency and power density. On the other hand, Asynchronous Motor operates by inducing currents in the rotor through electromagnetic induction, making it less expensive but slightly less efficient compared to synchronous motors. Both motors are widely used in electric vehicles for propulsion and offer different benefits based on the specific application.</p></p>
