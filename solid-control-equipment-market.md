Shale shakers are used to separate solids from drilling fluid, Mud Centrifuges are used for fine solids separation, Desander & Desilter remove sand and silt from drilling fluid, Mud Cleaners combine desanders and desilters for efficient solid removal, and Others include equipment such as degassers and agitators. Each type plays a crucial role in the drilling process by effectively managing solids to ensure smooth operations.</p></p>
