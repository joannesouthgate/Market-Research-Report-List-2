<p><p>Wind turbine lubricants market can be divided into two main types - synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oils are made from chemical synthesis and offer enhanced performance and durability compared to mineral oils. They are commonly used in high-performance wind turbines for better efficiency and protection against wear and corrosion. On the other hand, mineral oils are derived from naturally occurring crude oil and are suitable for less demanding applications. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operation and maintenance of wind turbines.</p></p>
