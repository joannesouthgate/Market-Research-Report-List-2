<p><strong>The Air Intake Resonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Resonators</li><li>Side Branch Resonators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air intake resonators are devices used to reduce intake noise and improve engine performance. In-Line resonators are placed directly in the air intake system, reducing noise without restricting airflow. Side branch resonators are attached to the side of the intake tube, allowing for noise reduction while minimizing space requirements. Both types of resonators serve the same purpose of enhancing engine performance by controlling intake noise levels, with each type offering unique benefits to suit different vehicle engine setups and designs.</p></p>
