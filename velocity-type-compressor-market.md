<p><p>Velocity type compressors are commonly categorized into three main types: Axial Compressors, Centrifugal Compressors, and Francis Compressors. Axial compressors use rotating airfoils to continuously compress air in the axial direction. Centrifugal compressors, on the other hand, use centrifugal force to compress air radially. Francis compressors combine features of both axial and radial compressors to achieve high efficiency. Each type of compressor caters to different operational requirements and applications in industries such as oil & gas, power generation, and manufacturing.</p></p>
