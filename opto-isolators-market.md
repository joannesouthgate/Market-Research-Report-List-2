<p><strong>The Opto Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polarization Dependent Opto-Isolators</li><li>Polarization Independent Opto-Isolators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Opto-Isolators market consists of two main types: Polarization Dependent Opto-Isolators and Polarization Independent Opto-Isolators. Polarization Dependent Opto-Isolators are sensitive to the input light's polarization, making them suitable for applications where polarization control is necessary. On the other hand, Polarization Independent Opto-Isolators are not affected by the input light's polarization, offering more flexibility in various optical systems. Both types cater to different requirements and play a crucial role in ensuring signal isolation and integrity in optoelectronic devices.</p></p>
