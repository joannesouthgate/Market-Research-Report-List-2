<p><p>Energy Saving Glass comes in different types to improve energy efficiency in buildings. Hollow glass consists of two or more panes separated by a space filled with gas, which reduces heat transfer. Vacuum glass has a vacuum-sealed space between the panes, further enhancing insulation. Both types help to reduce heat loss and save energy by improving the thermal performance of windows. These innovative technologies are becoming increasingly popular in the construction industry as they offer cost-effective solutions for energy conservation.</p></p>
