<p><p>Lead Zinc Titanates, Lead Titanate, and Lead Magnesium Niobate are different types of piezoelectric ceramic wafers used in various industrial applications. Lead Zinc Titanates offer high dielectric constant and excellent piezoelectric properties. Lead Titanate is known for its high electromechanical coupling coefficient, making it suitable for sensors and actuators. Lead Magnesium Niobate wafers are preferred for their high electromechanical properties and stability over a wide range of temperatures. Each type of ceramic wafer caters to specific requirements in different industries, providing unique benefits to the users.</p></p>
