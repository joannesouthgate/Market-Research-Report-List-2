<p><p>Fabric ducting market types can be categorized into four main segments: round fabric ducts, half-round fabric ducts, quarter-round fabric ducts, and oval fabric ducts. Round fabric ducts are the most common type and are ideal for high air volume applications. Half-round fabric ducts are used in low clearance spaces where traditional ductwork cannot be installed. Quarter-round fabric ducts are designed for narrow and confined spaces. Oval fabric ducts are used in large spaces where a high air distribution is required. Each type offers specific advantages based on the application requirements.</p></p>
