<p><strong>The Packaging Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexography Printing</li><li>Rotogravure Printing</li><li>Offset Printing</li><li>Digital Printing</li><li>Screen Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packaging printing market includes various types such as Flexography Printing, Rotogravure Printing, Offset Printing, Digital Printing, and Screen Printing. Flexography Printing uses flexible relief plates and is suitable for high-volume printing. Rotogravure Printing involves engraving the image onto a cylinder and is commonly used for long print runs. Offset Printing transfers ink onto a rubber sheet before applying it to the packaging material. Digital Printing offers quick turnaround and customization options. Screen Printing involves pressing ink through a mesh stencil onto the packaging material.</p></p>
