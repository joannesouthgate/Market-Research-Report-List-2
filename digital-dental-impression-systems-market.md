<p><p>The standalone digital dental impression systems are plug-and-play intraoral scanners that are self-contained units used for capturing dental impressions. On the other hand, mobile/portable systems consist of intraoral scanners connected to tablets/screens for capturing impressions on the go. Standalone systems are typically more user-friendly and convenient for in-office use, while mobile/portable systems offer greater flexibility and mobility for dental professionals who require a more portable solution for capturing impressions in various locations.</p></p>
