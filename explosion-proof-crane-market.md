<p><p>Explosion proof crane market offers two main types - Single Girder Overhead Crane and Double Girder Overhead Crane. Single Girder Overhead Crane is designed with a single bridge girder supported on either end and is suitable for lighter lifting requirements. Double Girder Overhead Crane, on the other hand, features two bridge girders and is ideal for heavier lifting capacities. Both types are specifically engineered to operate safely in hazardous environments where there is a risk of explosions, making them essential equipment in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and manufacturing.</p></p>
