<p><p>The Mobile ANPR Systems market comprises three main types of products: ANPR Cameras, ANPR Software, and Frame Grabbers. ANPR Cameras are used to capture license plate images, while ANPR Software processes and reads these images to extract relevant information. Frame Grabbers help in capturing and converting analog video signals to digital format for further processing. Triggers are used to activate the ANPR system, such as when a vehicle passes by a designated checkpoint. Each of these components plays a crucial role in the efficient functioning of mobile ANPR systems.</p></p>
