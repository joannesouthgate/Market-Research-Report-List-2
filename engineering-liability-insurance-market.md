<p><p>Engineering Liability Insurance can be divided into two main types: D&O Insurance and E&O Insurance. D&O Insurance, also known as Directors and Officers Insurance, provides coverage for actions taken by directors and officers of a company that may result in financial losses for the organization. E&O Insurance, or Errors and Omissions Insurance, protects professionals in the engineering industry from claims of negligence or inadequate work performance. Both types of insurance are essential for engineering firms to protect themselves from potential lawsuits and financial liabilities.</p></p>
