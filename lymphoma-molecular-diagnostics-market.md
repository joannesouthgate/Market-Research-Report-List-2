<p><p>Lymphoma molecular diagnostics market includes various types of technologies such as PCR, FISH, DNA sequencing, and gene chip. PCR (polymerase chain reaction) is used to amplify and analyze small amounts of DNA. FISH (fluorescence in situ hybridization) identifies specific genetic abnormalities. DNA sequencing determines the exact sequence of DNA molecules. Gene chips analyze gene expression patterns. These technologies play a crucial role in the diagnosis and prognosis of lymphoma, assisting in personalized treatment plans for patients.</p></p>
