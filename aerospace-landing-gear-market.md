<p><p>The Aerospace Landing Gear Market consists of two main types: Main Landing Gear and Nose/Tail Landing Gear. Nose/Tail Landing Gear, on the other hand, are smaller sets of landing gear located under the nose (for nose landing gear) or tail (for tail landing gear) of an aircraft. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring a safe and smooth landing for an aircraft.</p></p>
