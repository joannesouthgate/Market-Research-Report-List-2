<p><p>DTH Drilling Rig, also known as Down-the-Hole Drilling Rig, is a type of drilling equipment that utilizes a hammer-like mechanism to drill into rock formations. DTH drilling rigs are commonly used in mining, construction, and oil and gas exploration.</p><p>The DTH Drilling Rig Market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 5.2%.
