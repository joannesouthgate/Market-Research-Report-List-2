<p><p>Transparent concrete, also known as translucent concrete or light-transmitting concrete, comes in two main types: Optical fibers and concrete, and fine materials only (without coarse aggregates). The optical fibers and concrete type incorporates optical fibers into the concrete mix, allowing light to pass through. Both types offer unique aesthetic possibilities for architectural design and construction.</p></p>
