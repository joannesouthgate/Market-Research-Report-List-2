<p><strong>The Automatic Riveting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impact Riveting</li><li>Orbital Riveting</li><li>Radial(Spiralform) Riveting</li><li>Rollerform Riveting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic riveting machines are used in various industries for different applications. Impact riveting machines use a high-speed hammering action to join materials together efficiently. Orbital riveting machines create a smooth, uniform joint by rotating the rivet around the joint. Radial (spiralform) riveting machines form a spiral pattern around the joint for strong and secure connections. Rollerform riveting machines use rollers to press rivets into place, creating a tight seal. Each type of riveting machine caters to specific needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
