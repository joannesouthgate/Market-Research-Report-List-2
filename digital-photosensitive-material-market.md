<p><p>Digital photosensitive material market can be divided into two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) Chip and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) Chip. </p><p>CCD chips are known for their high-quality image resolution and sensitivity, making them ideal for scientific, medical, and industrial applications. On the other hand, CMOS chips are more cost-effective and energy-efficient, making them popular in consumer electronics, such as smartphones and digital cameras. Both types of chips play a crucial role in capturing and processing digital images for various purposes.</p></p>
