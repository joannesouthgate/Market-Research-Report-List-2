<p><p>Lead acid battery market is segmented into VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) battery, flooded battery, and other types of batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed batteries that are maintenance-free and designed for UPS systems and solar applications. Flooded batteries require regular maintenance and are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. Other types of lead acid batteries include gel batteries and AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries, which offer different performance characteristics and are used in various applications depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
