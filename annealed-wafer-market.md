<p><strong>The Annealed Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Argon Annealed Wafer</li><li>Hydrogen Annealed Wafer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Annealed wafer market consists of two main types: argon annealed wafer and hydrogen annealed wafer. Argon annealed wafer is treated in an argon atmosphere to reduce impurities and defects in the crystal structure, resulting in improved electrical performance. On the other hand, hydrogen annealed wafer is treated with hydrogen gas to passivate dangling bonds, enhancing the wafer's efficiency. Both types serve the purpose of enhancing the quality and performance of semiconductor wafers, catering to the needs of various industries such as electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
