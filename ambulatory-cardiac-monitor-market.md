<p><p>Ambulatory Cardiac Monitor market includes various types such as Holter Monitor, Event Monitors, Mobile Cardiac Telemetry, and other monitors. Holter Monitor records heart activity for a continuous period, typically 24-48 hours. Event Monitors are activated by the patient to record specific symptoms. Mobile Cardiac Telemetry devices continuously monitor heart activity and automatically transmit data to healthcare providers. Other types of monitors may include implantable devices or patch monitors. These monitors are crucial in diagnosing and monitoring cardiac conditions.</p></p>
