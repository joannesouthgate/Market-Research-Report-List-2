<p><p>Subsea processing systems are classified into four main types based on their function and purpose: Subsea Boosting System, Subsea Separation System, Subsea Injection System, and Subsea Compression System. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing the efficiency and productivity of subsea oil and gas operations. Subsea Boosting Systems increase the flow of hydrocarbons, Subsea Separation Systems separate oil, gas, and water at the seabed, Subsea Injection Systems inject chemicals or water into the reservoir, and Subsea Compression Systems compress gas to maintain flow rates.</p></p>
