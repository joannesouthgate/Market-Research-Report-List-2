Wimax (Worldwide Interoperability for Microwave Access) offers broadband wireless access over longer distances. Satellite technology enables communication across long distances, especially in remote areas. Wireless market includes various technologies for wireless communication, such as Bluetooth, WiFi, and NFC.</p></p>
