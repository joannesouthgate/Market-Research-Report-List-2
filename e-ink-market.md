<p><strong>The E Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Pigment E-Ink</li><li>Three Pigment E-Ink</li><li>Four Pigment E-Ink</li><li>Multi-Pigment E-Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Ink market types refer to the different types of electronic ink technologies used in e-readers and electronic displays. Two pigment E-Ink uses black and white particles to create text and images. Three pigment E-Ink adds a color filter to create limited color displays. Four pigment E-Ink expands the color range with four base colors. Finally, multi-pigment E-Ink utilizes a wider range of colors by incorporating more than four pigments. These technologies cater to different display needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
