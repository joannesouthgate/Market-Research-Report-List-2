<p><p>A smoke damper is a type of fire protection device used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems to prevent the spread of smoke in the event of a fire. When smoke is detected, the damper closes to prevent smoke from circulating through the ductwork and into other areas of a building. Smoke dampers are essential for ensuring the safety of building occupants and reducing property damage in the event of a fire.</p><p>The Smoke Damper Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period.
