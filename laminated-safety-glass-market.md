<p><strong>The Laminated Safety Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-based Laminated Safety Glass</li><li>Resin-based Laminated Safety Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminated safety glass is classified into two main types: polymer-based and resin-based. Polymer-based laminated safety glass is made with a layer of polymer interlayer between two pieces of glass, providing strength and flexibility. Resin-based laminated safety glass is created by bonding two glass sheets with a resin interlayer, offering durability and impact resistance. Both types of laminated safety glass enhance safety by keeping the glass intact when shattered, reducing the risk of injury from sharp glass fragments.</p></p>
