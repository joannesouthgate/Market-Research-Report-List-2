<p><strong>The Cosmetic Laminated Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic laminated tube packaging comes in two main types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes consist of a layer of aluminum sandwiched between layers of plastic, providing excellent barrier properties against oxygen and light. PBL tubes, on the other hand, use multiple layers of plastic with barrier properties added to one of the layers. Both types offer effective protection for cosmetic products, with ABL being preferred for more sensitive formulations and PBL providing a cost-effective alternative.</p></p>
