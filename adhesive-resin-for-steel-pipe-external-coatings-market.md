<p><p>Adhesive resin for steel pipe external coatings market encompasses various types, with two prominent ones being thermoplastic coatings and fusion bonded epoxy coatings. Thermoplastic coatings are applied in a molten state and form a strong bond upon cooling, providing excellent corrosion resistance and flexibility. Fusion bonded epoxy coatings involve a powder coating that is heat-cured onto the steel surface, forming a hard and durable protective layer. Both types offer effective protection against corrosion and abrasion for steel pipes in various applications.</p></p>
