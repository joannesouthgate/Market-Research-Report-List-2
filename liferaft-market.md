<p><ul><li>Foldable Liferaft</li><li>Un-foldable Liferaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foldable liferafts are designed to be compact and easily stored on board a vessel, making them ideal for smaller boats or yachts with limited space. They can be quickly deployed in case of an emergency, providing a safe escape for passengers. Un-foldable liferafts, on the other hand, are typically larger and more durable, suitable for commercial vessels or offshore rigs. They offer a greater capacity and stability, making them suitable for longer voyages or rough sea conditions. Both types provide essential safety measures for passengers in the event of a maritime disaster.</p></p>
