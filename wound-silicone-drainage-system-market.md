<p><p>Wound Silicone Drainage System market types include Open Wound Drainage System and Closed Wound Drainage System. Open Wound Drainage System involves a drain placed in the wound to allow fluid to flow out into a collection device. Closed Wound Drainage System involves a drain connected to a vacuum device that helps remove fluid from the wound and collect it in a container. Both systems are used to promote healing and prevent infection in surgical wounds or other types of wounds.</p></p>
