<p><p>A water heater is a household appliance that is used to heat water for various domestic purposes such as bathing, washing, and cooking. It works by heating cold water using a heating element and storing it in a tank for later use. There are different types of water heaters available in the market, including tankless water heaters, solar water heaters, heat pump water heaters, and conventional storage water heaters.</p><p>The Water Heater Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period. The market growth is being driven by factors such as increasing demand for hot water in residential and commercial buildings, rising awareness about energy-efficient products, and technological advancements in water heating systems. Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative water heaters that are energy-efficient, eco-friendly, and cost-effective.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the water heater market is the growing popularity of tankless water heaters. These units are more energy-efficient compared to traditional storage water heaters as they only heat water when it is needed, thereby reducing energy consumption and lowering operating costs. Additionally, consumers are increasingly looking for smart water heaters that can be controlled remotely via a smartphone app, leading to the integration of IoT technology in water heating systems.</p></p>
