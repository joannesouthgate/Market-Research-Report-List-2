<p><p>The process gas compressor market consists of four main types: centrifugal, reciprocating, screw, and others. Centrifugal compressors use rotating impellers to increase gas pressure, while reciprocating compressors use pistons to achieve the same result. Screw compressors rely on screws to compress gas, and other types may include axial or rotary compressors. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with centrifugal compressors often used for high flow rates and low-pressure ratios, while reciprocating compressors are more suited for high-pressure ratios and variable flow rates.</p></p>
