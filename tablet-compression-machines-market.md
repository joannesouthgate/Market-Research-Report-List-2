<p><ul><li>Single Station Type</li><li>Multiple Station Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet compression machines are categorized into two main types based on the number of stations: Single Station Type and Multiple Station Type. Single station machines are designed to compress tablets one at a time, making them suitable for small-scale production or research purposes. Multiple station machines, on the other hand, have several compression stations working simultaneously, increasing the production capacity significantly. These machines are ideal for large-scale manufacturing operations where high output is required. Both types play a crucial role in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries for tablet production.</p></p>
