<p><p>Composite windows and doors refer to products made from a combination of materials such as fiber reinforced plastics (FRP) and wood plastic composites (WPC). FRP offers high strength and durability, making it ideal for exterior applications. WPC, on the other hand, combines wood fibers and plastic to create a weather-resistant and low-maintenance option. Both types of composite materials are gaining popularity in the market for their ability to provide the benefits of traditional materials while offering improved performance and longevity.</p></p>
