<p><p>Hair creams are commonly categorized into three main market types: water-in-oil (W/O), oil-in-water (O/W), and other market types. O/W hair creams, on the other hand, have a higher water content, making them lighter in texture and suitable for normal to oily hair. Other market types include hybrid formulations or specialty hair creams designed for specific hair concerns such as frizz control or color protection.</p></p>
