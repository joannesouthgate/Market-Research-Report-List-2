<p><p>Gait rehabilitation exoskeletons come in two main types: single joint and multiple joint systems. Single joint exoskeletons focus on assisting movement in one specific joint, such as the knee or ankle, providing targeted support for individuals with limited mobility or muscle weakness. On the other hand, multiple joint exoskeletons offer assistance in more than one joint, offering a more comprehensive solution for gait rehabilitation. Both types aim to improve mobility, balance, and overall walking ability in patients undergoing rehabilitation therapy.</p></p>
