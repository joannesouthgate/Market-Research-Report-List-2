<p><p>Chemical Polishing Slurry Market is segmented into three main types: Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurries. </p><p>- Alumina Slurry: It is commonly used for the polishing of hard materials like metals and ceramics due to its high hardness and wear resistance.</p><p>- Colloidal Silica Slurry: This type is preferred for polishing applications requiring a fine finish, as it provides uniform particle size distribution for smooth surface polishing.</p><p>- Ceria Slurries: Ceria-based slurries are used for polishing glass, optics, and semiconductors due to their high removal rates and selectivity.</p></p>
