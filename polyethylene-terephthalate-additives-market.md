<p><p>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) additives Market Types include Color Addition, which is used to enhance the appearance of PET products. Ultraviolet Light Barrier additives protect PET products from UV degradation. Oxygen and Acetaldehyde Scavenging additives help extend the shelf life of PET packaging. Flame Resistance additives improve the fire safety of PET products. Other additives are used for various purposes such as impact resistance, thermal stability, and barrier properties.</p></p>
