<p><p>Smart gas and electricity meters are classified into two main market types: single-phase smart meters and three-phase smart meters. Single-phase smart meters are suitable for residential buildings and small businesses, as they measure energy consumption for single-phase electrical systems. On the other hand, three-phase smart meters are designed for larger commercial and industrial buildings, where energy consumption needs to be monitored across three phases of electrical systems. Both types of smart meters offer benefits such as real-time data monitoring, accurate billing, and improved energy efficiency.</p></p>
