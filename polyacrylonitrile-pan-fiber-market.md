<p><p>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) Fiber Market Types refer to the two common methods used in the production of PAN fibers - Wet Spinning and Dry Spinning. Wet spinning involves dissolving PAN in a solvent and extruding it into a coagulating bath to form fibers, while dry spinning involves dissolving PAN in a solvent and spinning it directly into a heated chamber to evaporate the solvent and solidify the fiber. Each method has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
