<p><p>Acrylic color markets can be segmented into three main categories based on the quality and price range: Artist Level, Master Level, and Beginner Level. </p><p>Artist Level acrylic colors are high-quality, professional-grade paints with a wide range of colors and excellent lightfastness. They are typically more expensive and are used by experienced artists who require the best performance and color accuracy.</p><p>Master Level acrylic colors are a step below Artist Level in terms of quality and price. They still offer good color pigmentation and durability, making them a popular choice for intermediate-level artists.</p><p>Beginner Level acrylic colors are the most affordable option and are designed for those new to painting or hobbyists. While they may not offer the same level of color intensity or longevity as Artist or Master Level paints, they are a great starting point for beginners to practice and experiment with different techniques.</p></p>
