<p><ul><li>Low Pass Analog Filters</li><li>High Pass Analog Filters</li><li>Band Pass Filters</li><li>Notch Filters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog filter market can be categorized into different types based on their functionality. Low pass analog filters allow low frequency signals to pass through while attenuating high frequency signals. High pass analog filters do the opposite, allowing high frequency signals to pass through while attenuating low frequency signals. Band pass filters allow a specific range of frequencies to pass through, while notch filters attenuate a specific frequency. There are also others types of analog filters available in the market catering to different frequency filtering requirements.</p></p>
