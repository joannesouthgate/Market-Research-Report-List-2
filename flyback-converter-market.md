<p><p>Flyback converters are commonly used in power supply applications due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. There are two main types of flyback converters: isolated and non-isolated. Isolated flyback converters provide electrical isolation between input and output circuits, making them suitable for high voltage applications. On the other hand, non-isolated flyback converters do not provide isolation but are more compact and efficient. Both types of flyback converters are widely used in various industries for powering electronics and appliances.</p></p>
