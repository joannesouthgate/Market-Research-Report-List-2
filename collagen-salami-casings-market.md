<p><p>Collagen salami casings come in two main types: Edible collagen casings and non-edible collagen casings. Edible collagen casings are made from edible collagen derived from animal hides or bones, and are consumed along with the salami. They provide a natural casing that is easy to peel off and adds flavor to the salami. Non-edible collagen casings, on the other hand, are used purely for packaging purposes and are removed before consumption. They provide a convenient and cost-effective alternative to traditional casings.</p></p>
