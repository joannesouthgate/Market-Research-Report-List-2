<p><p>An Ultrasonic Retro-reflective Sensor is a type of sensor that uses ultrasonic waves to detect the presence or absence of an object. This type of sensor is commonly used in industrial automation, automotive applications, and security systems.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Retro-reflective Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period.
