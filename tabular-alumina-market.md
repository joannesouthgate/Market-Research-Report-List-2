Coarse Tabular Alumina consists of larger particle sizes, typically used for applications requiring high mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance. On the other hand, Fine Tabular Alumina is composed of smaller particle sizes, suitable for applications where smooth surface finishes and precise dimensional control are needed.
