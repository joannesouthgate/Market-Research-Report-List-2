<p><ul><li>Single Freewheel</li><li>Multilevel Flywheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flywheels market is comprised of two main types: single freewheel and multilevel flywheel. Single freewheel systems consist of a single flywheel connected to the engine and transmission to store rotational energy. On the other hand, multilevel flywheel systems utilize multiple flywheels connected in series to provide enhanced energy storage capabilities. Both types play a vital role in improving the efficiency and performance of vehicles by storing and releasing energy during acceleration and deceleration.</p></p>
