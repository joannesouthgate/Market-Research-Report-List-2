<p><p>Laparoscopic Tumor Ablation market includes various types of technologies used for destroying tumors. Microwave Ablation uses electromagnetic waves to heat and destroy the tumor cells. Radiofrequency (RF) Ablation uses high-frequency electrical current to kill the tumor cells. Chemical Ablation involves injecting chemicals directly into the tumor to eliminate it. Cryotherapy freezes and destroys the tumor cells. Other types may include laser ablation, high-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU), and irreversible electroporation (IRE). These technologies provide minimally invasive options for tumor treatment.</p></p>
