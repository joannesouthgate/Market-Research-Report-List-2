End-Banded Chip Thermistors have a resistive element with metal caps at both ends, providing stability in harsh environments. MELF Style Thermistors have a cylindrical shape with metal ends for enhanced heat dissipation. Top/Bottom Terminated Chip Thermistors have terminations on the top and bottom for increased flexibility in circuit design. Each type offers unique features catering to specific application requirements.</p></p>
