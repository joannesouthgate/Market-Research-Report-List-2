<p><p>Self-protected MOSFETs are commonly categorized into two main types based on their application in the circuit: Low Side Devices and High Side Devices. Low Side Devices are used to control the current on the ground side of the load, while High Side Devices are used to control the current on the supply side of the load. Both types of self-protected MOSFETs offer protection features such as overcurrent, overtemperature, and short-circuit protection, making them ideal for a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
