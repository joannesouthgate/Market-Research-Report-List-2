<p><p>The Laminated Core Fixed Inductor market can be classified into two types: Multi-layer Type and Wire Wound Type. Multi-layer Type inductors consist of multiple layers of winding coils separated by insulating layers, providing high inductance and performance. Wire Wound Type inductors are made by winding a conductive wire around a magnetic core, offering a cost-effective solution with good magnetic properties. Both types cater to different application needs and are widely used in various electronic devices for energy storage and signal filtering purposes.</p></p>
