<p><p>Liquid gel packs can be divided into two main types based on their temperature properties: cold gel packs and hot gel packs. Cold gel packs are designed to provide cooling relief for injuries, inflammation, and fevers by absorbing heat from the body. On the other hand, hot gel packs are used to provide soothing warmth for muscle aches, cramps, and stiffness by emitting heat when activated. Both types of gel packs are commonly used in medical settings, sports therapy, and for general pain relief.</p></p>
