<p><p>The Digital Urine Meter market consists of two main types: Single Function and Multifunction meters. Single Function meters are designed for measuring only one aspect of urine output, such as volume. On the other hand, Multifunction meters are equipped with multiple features, such as measuring volume, flow rate, temperature, and specific gravity of urine. Single Function meters are typically more affordable and simpler to use, while Multifunction meters offer a wider range of monitoring capabilities for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
