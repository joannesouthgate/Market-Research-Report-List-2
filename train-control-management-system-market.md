<p><p>Train Control Management Systems (TCMS) market includes Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC), Positive Train Control (PTC), and Integrated Train Control systems. CBTC uses communication technology to control train movements, improving safety and capacity. PTC is designed to prevent accidents and collisions by monitoring train locations and speeds. Integrated Train Control systems combine various control functions for seamless operation. These technologies are crucial for ensuring efficient and safe train operations, and are increasingly being adopted by rail networks worldwide.</p></p>
