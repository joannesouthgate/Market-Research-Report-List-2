<p><p>Hexane is a widely used solvent with three main market types: N-hexane, Isohexane, and Neohexane. N-hexane is a straight-chain isomer that is primarily used in industrial applications such as extraction and cleaning processes. Isohexane is an isomer with branched chains, often used in the production of gasoline and as a solvent in various industries. Neohexane is another isomer used in the production of chemicals like plasticizers and perfumes. Each type of hexane serves specific purposes in different sectors of the market.</p></p>
