<p><p>Burnt Dolomite is classified into two main types based on the level of burning process: Dead Burnt Dolomite and Light Burnt Dolomite. Dead Burnt Dolomite is produced by heating the mineral at high temperatures to remove any impurities, resulting in a material with high density and low reactivity. On the other hand, Light Burnt Dolomite undergoes a less intense burning process, retaining some of its reactivity. Both types have various applications in industries such as steelmaking, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
