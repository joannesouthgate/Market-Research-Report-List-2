<p><p>Terahertz imaging cameras can be classified into two main types: Passive Terahertz Imaging and Active Terahertz Imaging. </p><p>Passive Terahertz Imaging involves detecting naturally occurring terahertz radiation emitted by objects, while Active Terahertz Imaging uses a terahertz source to actively emit radiation onto objects and then detect the reflected signals. </p><p>Passive imaging is typically used for security screening and surveillance applications, while active imaging is more commonly used in industrial and scientific research for materials testing and quality control purposes.</p></p>
