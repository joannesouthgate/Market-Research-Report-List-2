<p><p>Superabsorbent Polymers (SAP) come in three main types: Starch-based SAP, Cellulose-based SAP, and Acrylic Resin SAP. Starch-based SAP is derived from natural starch sources and is biodegradable, making it environmentally friendly. Cellulose-based SAP is made from cellulose fibers and is also biodegradable. Acrylic Resin SAP is the most commonly used type and is non-biodegradable, but has high absorbency and is cost-effective. Each type of SAP offers unique properties and benefits for various applications in industries such as hygiene products, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
