<p><p>Austenitic Alloys Tubes Market consists of two main types: Seamless Tubes and Welded Tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured without any welding seams, resulting in a smoother and cleaner surface finish, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Welded tubes, on the other hand, are made by welding strips of metal together to form a tube. They are more cost-effective than seamless tubes but may not be suitable for high-pressure applications due to the presence of weld seams.</p></p>
