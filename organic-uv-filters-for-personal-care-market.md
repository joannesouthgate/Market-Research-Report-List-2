<p><p>Organic UV filters for personal care products are categorized into two main types: water phase and oil phase filters. Water phase filters are water-soluble and used in formulations like lotions and creams. They provide protection against UV rays while maintaining a light and non-greasy texture. Oil phase filters are oil-soluble and are commonly used in products like sunscreens and facial oils. They offer effective UV protection and are suitable for products with higher oil content. Both types are essential for formulating sun protection products for various skin types and preferences.</p></p>
