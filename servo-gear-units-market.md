<p><p>Servo Gear Units are classified into two main types: Linear Servo Gearbox and Right Angle Servo Gearbox. Linear Servo Gearboxes are designed to provide linear motion, making them ideal for applications where precise positioning and high-speed operation are required. On the other hand, Right Angle Servo Gearboxes are designed to provide right angle motion, making them suitable for applications where space constraints are a concern. Both types of servo gear units offer high precision, efficiency, and durability, making them essential components in various industrial machinery and automation systems.</p></p>
