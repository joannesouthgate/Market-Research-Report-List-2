Piezocrystals are natural or synthetic materials that generate a voltage when mechanically stressed. Piezoceramics are the most commonly used type due to their high sensitivity and durability. Piezopolymers are flexible and lightweight, making them suitable for diverse applications. Piezocomposites combine multiple materials to enhance performance and efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages for various industries and applications.</p></p>
