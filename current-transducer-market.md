<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Current transducers are devices used to measure electric currents in circuits and are classified into two main types - Open Loop and Closed Loop. </p><p>Open Loop current transducers do not require the conductor to pass through the sensor loop, making them easy to install and maintain. However, they are less accurate and susceptible to external interference. </p><p>Closed Loop current transducers provide higher accuracy by requiring the conductor to pass through the sensor loop, but they are more complex and expensive to install and maintain.</p></p>
