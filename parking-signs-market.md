<p><p>Parking signs can be classified into two main market types: Traditional and LED. Traditional parking signs are typically made of metal or plastic and display information using printed text and symbols. These signs are commonly used in older parking lots and have been the standard for many years. On the other hand, LED parking signs feature light-emitting diodes that can display dynamic information such as available parking spaces and pricing. LED signs are considered more modern and efficient, making them popular in newer parking facilities.</p></p>
