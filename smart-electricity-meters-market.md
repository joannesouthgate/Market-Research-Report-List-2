<p><p>Smart electricity meters are available in two main types: single phase and three phase. Single phase meters are typically used in residential settings where electricity is supplied through a single phase. Three phase meters are used in commercial and industrial settings where electricity is supplied through three phases. Single phase meters are simpler and less expensive, while three phase meters are more complex and able to handle higher voltages. Both types of meters offer advanced monitoring and communication capabilities to help consumers and utility companies better manage electricity usage.</p></p>
