<p><p>Thermal imagers are used in various industries for applications such as night vision, security, and medical diagnostics. There are two main types of thermal imagers in the market: the VOx (vanadium oxide) and a-Si (amorphous silicon) thermal imagers. VOx thermal imagers are known for their high sensitivity and thermal resolution, making them ideal for military and industrial applications. On the other hand, a-Si thermal imagers are more cost-effective and commonly used in consumer electronics and automotive industries. Both types of thermal imagers play a vital role in enhancing safety and efficiency in diverse sectors.</p></p>
