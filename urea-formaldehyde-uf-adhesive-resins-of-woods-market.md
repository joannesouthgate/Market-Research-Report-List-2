<p><p>Urea-Formaldehyde (UF) Adhesive Resins are commonly used in the wood industry for bonding purposes. There are two main types of UF resins in the market: Type I and Type II. Type I resins are designed for interior applications where exposure to moisture is limited, while Type II resins are suitable for exterior applications with higher moisture resistance. Both types offer strong bonding properties and are widely used in the production of wood products such as plywood, particleboard, and MDF.</p></p>
