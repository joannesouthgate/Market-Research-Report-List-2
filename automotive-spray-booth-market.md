<p><p>Automotive spray booths are essential for painting vehicles efficiently and safely. There are different types of automotive spray booths available in the market. Cross flow paint booths have a horizontal airflow that moves paint fumes away from the vehicle. Down draft paint booths pull air from the ceiling down to the floor, removing overspray. Side down draft paint booths have a similar concept but with airflow coming from the sides. Other types of spray booths may include semi-downdraft or reverse flow booths, each with their own unique features and benefits.</p></p>
