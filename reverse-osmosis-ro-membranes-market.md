<p><p>Reverse Osmosis (RO) membranes are essential components in water filtration systems, commonly used in various industries and households. There are mainly two types of RO membranes in the market - Cellulose base film and Thin film composite film. Cellulose base film membranes are made from natural materials and are cost-effective, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, Thin film composite film membranes are more durable and offer higher filtration efficiency, making them ideal for high-pressure water treatment systems.</p></p>
