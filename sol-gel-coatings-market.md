<p><ul><li>Resin Coatings</li><li>Inorganic Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sol-gel coatings market can be categorized into two main types: resin coatings and inorganic coatings. Resin coatings are formulated using organic polymers that provide excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them ideal for use on various surfaces such as metal, glass, and plastic. On the other hand, inorganic coatings are made from metal alkoxides and oxides, offering superior hardness and durability. These coatings are commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics for corrosion protection and thermal insulation.</p></p>
