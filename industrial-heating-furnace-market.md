<p><p>Industrial heating furnace market includes both combustion type and electric type furnaces. Combustion type furnaces use fuels such as natural gas, oil, or coal to produce heat through combustion processes. These furnaces are suitable for high temperature applications and are common in industries like metal processing. On the other hand, electric type furnaces use electricity as the heat source, making them more energy efficient and easier to control. They are commonly used in industries where precise temperature control is required, such as heat treating and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
