<p><p>Gelcoat fillers can be categorized into various types based on the resin used in the formulation. Polyester gelcoat fillers are cost-effective and commonly used in applications where high durability is not necessary. Epoxy gelcoat fillers provide excellent chemical and water resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments. Vinyl ester gelcoat fillers offer enhanced strength, impact resistance, and corrosion resistance compared to polyester options. Other types of gelcoat fillers exist, but these three are the most widely used in the market.</p></p>
