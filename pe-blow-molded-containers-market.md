<p><p>PE Blow Molded Containers are categorized into two main types based on the material used: HDPE and LDPE. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) containers are known for their strong and durable properties, making them suitable for packaging products that require high strength and rigidity. On the other hand, LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) containers are more flexible and have good tensile strength, making them ideal for packaging products that need flexibility and impact resistance. Both types of containers have their own unique characteristics and are used in various industries based on their specific properties.</p></p>
