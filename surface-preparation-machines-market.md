<p><p>Surface Preparation Machines are essential for cleaning and modifying surfaces before various processes such as coating or bonding. Atmospheric Pressure Plasma Surface Preparation Machines operate at normal atmospheric conditions, while Low Pressure / Vacuum Plasma Surface Preparation Machines work in a controlled low pressure or vacuum environment. Atmospheric Pressure machines are more commonly used for larger-scale applications, while Low Pressure machines are typically preferred for delicate or sensitive materials. Both types of machines help improve adhesion, durability, and overall quality of surface treatments.</p></p>
