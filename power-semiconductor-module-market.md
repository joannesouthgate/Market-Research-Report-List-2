<p><p>Power Semiconductor Modules are categorized into four main types: diodes, transistors, thyristors, and others. Diodes are used for unidirectional current flow, transistors for controlling current, and thyristors for switching and amplification. The "others" category includes different types of power semiconductor devices such as insulated gate bipolar transistor (IGBT), silicon-controlled rectifier (SCR), and metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistor (MOSFET). These modules are essential in various industries like automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics for efficient power management and control.</p></p>
