<p><p>Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors market is divided into two main types: Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) and Inertial Navigation System (INS). AHRS is designed to provide accurate information on an aircraft or vehicle's orientation, heading, and position, making it essential for flight control systems. On the other hand, INS utilizes AHRS data along with additional inputs to calculate precise position, velocity, and attitude of an object in motion, making it crucial for applications like autonomous vehicles and unmanned aerial vehicles.</p></p>
