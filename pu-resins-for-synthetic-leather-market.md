<p><strong>The PU Resins for Synthetic Leather Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-process Synthetic Leather</li><li>Wet-process Synthetic Leather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU resins are commonly used in the production of synthetic leather, which can be categorized into two main types: dry-process synthetic leather and wet-process synthetic leather. </p><p>Dry-process synthetic leather involves adding PU resins directly to the fabric through a dry process, resulting in a durable and high-quality material. </p><p>In contrast, wet-process synthetic leather entails coating the fabric with a mixture of PU resins, solvents, and additives before drying and finishing the material. </p><p>Both processes result in synthetic leather that mimics the look and feel of genuine leather.</p></p>
