<p><p>Black and white ultrasound devices are typically used for basic imaging purposes, providing a grayscale image of the internal organs and structures. While black and white ultrasound devices are more cost-effective and widely used for routine examinations, color ultrasound devices are preferred for more complex diagnostic procedures requiring detailed vascular mapping and flow analysis.</p></p>
