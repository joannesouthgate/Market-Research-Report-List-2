<p><strong>The Lab Diamond Ring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD Diamonds</li><li>HTHP Diamonds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab diamond rings come in two main types: CVD diamonds and HTHP diamonds. CVD diamonds are created using chemical vapor deposition, resulting in high-quality diamonds with minimal impurities. HTHP diamonds, on the other hand, are produced using high pressure and high temperature methods, resulting in diamonds that are visually identical to natural diamonds. Both types of lab diamonds are gaining popularity in the market due to their ethical sourcing and lower cost compared to natural diamonds.</p></p>
