<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene (GFRP) is used in two main markets: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. In Injection Molding, GFRP is blended with polypropylene and injected into a mold to create various products such as automotive parts, consumer goods, and industrial components. Extrusion Molding involves melting the GFRP blend and forcing it through a die to create continuous shapes like pipes, tubing, and profiles. Both markets benefit from the enhanced strength, stiffness, and heat resistance provided by the glass fibers in the polypropylene matrix.</p></p>
