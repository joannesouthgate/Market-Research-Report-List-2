ELISA-based assays detect host cell proteins using antibodies that bind to specific antigens, offering high throughput capabilities. Both methods are commonly used in biopharmaceutical manufacturing to ensure product purity and safety. Both play a critical role in ensuring the quality of biopharmaceutical products.</p></p>
