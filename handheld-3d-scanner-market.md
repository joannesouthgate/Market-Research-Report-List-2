<p><strong>The Handheld 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structure Light Scanner</li><li>Laser Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structure Light Scanner and Laser Scanner are two types of handheld 3D scanners used in various industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment. Structure Light Scanner uses a projector and camera to capture 3D data by projecting light patterns onto an object and measuring the distortions in the pattern to create a 3D model. Laser Scanner, on the other hand, uses laser beams to measure distances and create a 3D model by scanning the object point by point. Both types of scanners offer high precision and are used for various applications such as quality control, reverse engineering, and virtual reality.</p></p>
