<p><p>Paint guns are available in three main market types: Semi-Automatic, Fully-Automatic, and Manual. Semi-Automatic paint guns require the user to pull the trigger for each paint spray, offering more control and precision. Fully-Automatic paint guns continuously spray paint when the trigger is pulled, allowing for faster coverage but less control. Manual paint guns require the user to manually pump or pressurize the paint for each spray. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different painting applications.</p></p>
