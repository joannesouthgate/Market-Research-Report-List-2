<p><p>Transformer steel is classified based on its magnetic properties and silicon content, with types including 23Z, 27Z, 30Z, 35Z, 23ZH, 27ZH, 30ZH, 35ZH. The Z-grade steel has lower silicon content and lower magnetic permeability, making it suitable for high frequency applications. On the other hand, the ZH-grade steel has higher silicon content and higher magnetic permeability, making it ideal for low frequency power transformers. These classifications help manufacturers choose the right material based on the specific requirements of their transformer applications.</p></p>
